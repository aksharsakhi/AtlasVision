Frontend

  • ⚡ Vite + TypeScript — blazing fast build
  • 🗺️ Leaflet.js — 2D interactive maps
  • 🌍 CesiumJS — WebGL 3D globe
  • 🛰️ satellite.js — SGP4 orbit propagation
  • 📈 TradingView — live market charts

Backend (Node.js + Express)

  • ✈️ /api/flights — multi-source ADS-B aggregation
  • 🛰️ /api/tle — CelesTrak proxy (CORS bypass)
  • 📰 /api/rss — universal RSS feed proxy
  • 🔍 /api/flight-search — full fleet search
  • 📋 /api/flight/:id — per-flight details

🔒 Privacy — User Location

Your location (if permission granted) is used only locally in your browser to center maps and place a marker. It is never stored, sent to any server, or shared with third parties. Data stays within your device session.
